CICERO SLATER

Cicero is a typical college student attending Miskatonic South Satellite Campus(MSSC), until a car accident sends him crawling through a thicket in the woods fighting to stay alive. Now he's faced with a choice. Does he let the ancient in the woods sway him and keep himself breathing? Or does he forge through on his own and risk everything he holds dear?
